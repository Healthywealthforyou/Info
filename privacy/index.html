Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2025

Welcome to the Health Wearable App. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application, including features such as food photo uploads, nutrition tracking, AI-powered insights, and sharing with your chosen dietician.

1. Information We Collect

  • Photos and Images: You may upload images of your meals. These images are stored securely and analyzed using AI for nutritional insights.
  • Nutrition Data: Based on your logs and photos, the app may collect estimated data about your dietary intake (e.g., calories, protein, sugar, carbs, fats).
  • User Preferences: Settings, fasting goals, and meal tagging preferences.
  • Device Data: Basic anonymized info to support analytics and performance monitoring.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized nutrition insights using AI engines.
  • To allow you to log and view your diet across day/week/month/year.
  • To enable you to share meal logs and insights with your selected dietician.
  • To improve recommendations and app features based on aggregated trends.

3. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal information. You have control over which data is shared with your chosen dietician through app settings. Data shared is used strictly to support your health goals.

4. Your Rights & Choices

  • You may delete meal images and logs at any time.
  • You may disable dietician sharing or revoke permissions from the app settings.
  • You may contact us to request deletion of all stored data.

5. Data Security

We use modern encryption and secure infrastructure to store your data. AI processing occurs on secure servers and follows industry-standard best practices.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in-app and on our website with the updated effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at: support@healthwearableapp.com
